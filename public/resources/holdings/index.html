Where can I find other issues of the Egyptian Gazette?

Jul 22, 2020

This website offers very detailed digitization of a relatively narrow range of issues (1905-1908). But the Gazette published for many years. Where can you find other issues?

This is a summary of what I know is available. I’d love to learn more about what you know. Please contact me to contribute to this page.

Years Notes Links
1882-4 First year of publication. Scattered issues available on this site. See also CEAlex
1884-1892 Not clear where these issues can be found.
1893-1909 On microfilm at FSU and elsewhere FSU library catalog
1910-30 The British library holds these (and more) on microfilm: 31 Dec.1938; 1 March 1939 - 30 March 1940; 6 Jan. - 26 March, 13-16 April. 16 Oct 1941 - 20 May, 13 June 1943 - 29 Feb 1944, 3 Jan.1945 - 7 March, 1 June 1947 - Dec.1993; Jan.1998 - 31 Dec.2000 British Library catalog
1930-51 CRL holds this. Details: MF-346 FNMP r.1-33 1930-Jan 15, 1941; (LACKS: Mar 24-26, 1930; Nov 25, 1933 (pp. 3-4); Feb 19-21, May 7-9, Oct 10 (pp. 1-2, 7-8), 1934; Jun 8, 1935 (pp. 3-4); Jan 29 (pp. 3-4), Feb 11 (pp. 3-4), May 17 (pp. 3-4), Dec 25, 1937; Jun 23-25, Sep 22-25, Dec 5-7, 1938; Jan 8-10, May 10-11, Jun 6-8, 10-12, Jul 25-27, Oct 10-12, Nov 2, 1940.) r.33-38 Jan 3, 1943-1946, r.39-40/e 1947-Jun 1948., r.41-44 Jul 1948-Jun 1950, r.45/e Jul-Dec 1950, r.46-47 1951. CRL catalog
1952-66 Harvard holds Microfilm: MF-68 r.1-4 1952, r.5-8/e 1953, r.9-12 1954, r.13-16 1955. MF-75 FNMP r.1-4 1956, r.5-8 1957, r.9-12 1958, r.13-21 1959-Jun 1963, r.22/e Jul-Dec 1963, r.23-28 1964-1966. CRL catalog
1940s-60s CRL is posting copies online for certain periods: 1947-Jun 1948; Jul-Dec 1950; 1953; Jul-Dec 1963 CRL Digital Delivery
1967-94 CRL MF-75 FNMP r. 29-30 1967, r.31-42 1968-1973, MF-390 FNMP (20 reels) 1974-1983., MF-6486 (20 reels) 1984-Jun 1992, MF-9344 (22 reels) Jul 1992-Dec 1994; Jan-Dec 1998 CRL catalog
2000-2007 Library of Congress microfilm? CRL catalog
